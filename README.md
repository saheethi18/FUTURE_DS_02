📊 Customer Churn Analysis (Task Description)
📌 Objective
The objective of this task is to analyze customer churn behavior and identify key factors that influence customer retention. The focus is on deriving actionable insights that can help businesses reduce churn and improve customer satisfaction.
📁 Dataset Overview
The dataset used is Telco Customer Churn dataset, which includes:
Customer Details – tenure, contract type, payment method
Financial Data – MonthlyCharges, TotalCharges
Churn – Whether the customer left the service (Yes/No)
⚙️ Steps Performed
1️⃣ Data Loading & Exploration
Loaded dataset using pandas
Displayed first few rows and dataset structure
Checked for missing values
2️⃣ Data Cleaning
Converted TotalCharges to numeric format
Handled missing values by removing null entries
👉 Ensures accurate and reliable analysis.
3️⃣ Churn Analysis
📊 Churn Distribution
Visualized how many customers churned vs stayed
👉 Helps understand overall churn rate
📊 Churn by Contract Type
Compared churn across different contract types
👉 Month-to-month contracts show higher churn
📊 Churn by Payment Method
Analyzed churn behavior based on payment methods
👉 Certain payment methods are linked with higher churn
📊 Monthly Charges vs Churn
Compared monthly charges of churned vs retained customers
👉 Higher charges may influence churn
📊 Tenure vs Churn
Compared customer tenure with churn
👉 Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to churn
4️⃣ KPI Metrics
Retention Rate = Percentage of customers who stayed
Churn Rate = Percentage of customers who left
👉 These metrics provide a quick business performance overview.
📈 Key Insights
Customers with month-to-month contracts have higher churn
New customers (low tenure) are more likely to leave
Higher monthly charges can contribute to churn
Payment method plays a role in customer behavior
✅ Conclusion
This analysis highlights critical factors affecting customer churn. By focusing on contract types, pricing strategies, and early customer engagement, businesses can improve retention.
